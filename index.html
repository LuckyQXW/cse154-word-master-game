Word Master

Welcome to 2019 Word Master Competition!
This is a 2-player game where players take turns to give words that start with the given letters.
Lets find out who is the REAL Word Master!

Submit

Enter the word in the text box then click submit to show off your great storage of vocabulary! No repeat! You will have 5 seconds.

Challenge!

If you believe the word given by your opponent is not valid, you have 3 seconds to challenge them!

A player wins if:

  1. Opponent fails to give a valid word within time constraint
  2. Successfully challenges the word of their opponent
